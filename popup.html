Preferences

Collects usage

Reads your month-to-date usage from Claude, ChatGPT, and Cursor's own Usage pages via your signed-in browser sessions — no API keys. It reuses an open Usage tab or briefly opens and closes one; Cursor is shown for a moment because its dashboard won't load while hidden (scheduled refreshes do this only if enabled in Settings). Refreshes on your chosen interval (15 min default).

Tracks against budget

Each provider's spend is normalized against its monthly budget and combined; Cursor credits are converted to dollars.

Splits spend by period

The summary shows Today, Week (Mon–Fri), and Month spend. allowance is what's safe to spend today — budget left ÷ remaining workdays (bracket: ÷ all remaining days). proj is projected month-end at your current workday pace; left is budget minus month-to-date. Each provider row also shows its % remaining and average/available per workday.

Stays local

Budgets, snapshots, and history stay in browser storage; cookies, tokens, and raw responses are never saved. Settings can export or restore a local backup, replacing current data after confirmation.
