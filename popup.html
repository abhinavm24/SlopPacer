Collects account usage

Slop Pacer checks Claude, ChatGPT, and Cursor at your chosen interval (15 minutes by default) using your signed-in browser sessions. When needed, it briefly opens a Usage tab and closes only the tab it created. Cursor must be temporarily shown because its dashboard does not load usage data while hidden. Scheduled refreshes do this only when enabled in Settings.

Tracks month to date

Usage is normalized against each monthly budget. Cursor is always opened with a first-of-month through today date range.

Splits spend by period

The summary shows Today, Week (Mon–Fri), and Month spend. allowance is what is safe to spend today — the budget left this month divided by remaining workdays; the bracketed figure spreads it over all remaining days. proj projects month-end at your current workday pace, and left is the budget minus month-to-date.

Stays local

Budgets, snapshots, and history remain in browser storage. Settings lets you export a local backup or restore one, replacing current Slop Pacer data after confirmation.
